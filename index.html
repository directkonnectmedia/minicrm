    Rhythm setup

    Set billing anchors for finance (yellow) only when there are multiple financed payments (2+ installments or 2+ balance-due chunks). A single setup/lump payment skips yellow. Subscription (red) is unchanged. Due-today lines (blue) are never included.

    Billing calendar (simulation)

    Preview spans the next 6 months from today. Use ← → on the calendar to move between months. The legend explains solid colors and collision blends (e.g. orange = financing + subscription, brown = all three).

    Click a colored day for the total and line breakdown. Click again to dismiss.

    Payment philosophy

    How should automated billing behave for this client’s live account when real-world timing drifts (late payments, mid-month starts)? This does not change the plan template or invoice template.

    If a late payment lands too close to the next anchor charge, skip or delay that next charge to avoid double-billing.

    Minor delays under this threshold do not move future dates.
